RISE UP

Rise Up is a strategy-based game set during Modern Age.

A terrible plague has infected the world and only you can decide how to face this global threat. Choose carefully the best move and pay attention to your population in order to avoid riots or unpleasants outcomes. Everything will have a consequence, it's up to you whether to shine or to fall under the blows of the disease.

You will have full powers on every political and economical decision.

It's your turn now

Features

HEALTH

Build hospitals using the health funds and move beds from different specialities to the reanimate in order to give to the infects who need, the right healthcare and reduce the deaths, but be careful, if you move too many beds people will die for other reasons. Pay attention, more people die, more feeling will decrease

YOUR DECISIONS

Structure the whole political system of your country, choose between several opportunities everytime and be careful because the popolation will be influenced by every move you make. Organize also every economical problem and make loans with the European Central Bank with different amounts, interests and deadline in order to gain advantages!

YOUR FAULT

Your goal will be to face a period of crysis and to develop a strategy in order to Rise. You can choose between 3 different worlds of increasing difficulty. Everytime your pil will exceed the 30% of the initial, the whole population of the country gets infected or the feeling go to 0%, you lose. Good Luck!
